The biography of the sauti sol members

Bien Baraza

Bien was born in the year 1987.His musical journey has been a succesfull one.His role in the band is as song writer.Bien is married with one wife and happy.Since Sauti Sol’s inception in 2006, Bien-Aime has remained active in the group and part of the creation of Sauti Sol two albums: Mwanzo (2008) and Sol Filosofia (2011). Willis Chimano

He is the vocalist in the group .He is not yet married but very dedicated in the music industry.Chimano has been in the band since the year 2006 and has really brought him more fame and popularity.His deep voice and flamboyant style makes him stand out in the group. Before the formation of Sauti Sol, Chimano contested and won in the annual Fête de la Musique music competition at the French Cultural Centre, while studying French.
